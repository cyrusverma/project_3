Mac Book Pro

$1378.22

The MacBook Pro features a Touch Bar with an integrated Touch ID sensor and comes in two finishes: Silver and Space Gray. The Retina display measures 15.4 inches diagonally and is LED-backlit with IPS technology, boasting a native resolution of 2880 by 1800 at 220 pixels per inch and support for millions of colors. Supported scaled resolutions include 1920 by 1200, 1680 by 1050, 1280 by 800, and 1024 by 640. The display also offers 500 nits brightness, wide color (P3), and True Tone technology.

There are two processor options available: a 2.6GHz 6-core Intel Core i7 with Turbo Boost up to 4.5GHz and 12MB shared L3 cache, configurable to a 2.4GHz 8-core Intel Core i9 with Turbo Boost up to 5.0GHz and 16MB shared L3 cache, and a 2.3GHz 8-core Intel Core i9 with Turbo Boost up to 4.8GHz and 16MB shared L3 cache, configurable to a 2.4GHz 8-core Intel Core i9 with Turbo Boost up to 5.0GHz and 16MB shared L3 cache.

Storage options start at 256GB SSD, configurable to 512GB, 1TB, 2TB, or 4TB SSD, and a 512GB SSD, configurable to 1TB, 2TB, or 4TB SSD. The MacBook Pro comes with 16GB of 2400MHz DDR4 onboard memory, which is configurable to 32GB.

Graphics options for the 2.6GHz model include Radeon Pro 555X with 4GB of GDDR5 memory and automatic graphics switching, and Intel UHD Graphics 630, configurable to Radeon Pro 560X with 4GB of GDDR5 memory. For the 2.3GHz model, options include Radeon Pro 560X with 4GB of GDDR5 memory and automatic graphics switching, and Intel UHD Graphics 630, configurable to Radeon Pro Vega 16 with 4GB of HBM2 memory or Radeon Pro Vega 20 with 4GB of HBM2 memory.

The iPod Pro offers a compact yet powerful music experience, boasting a sleek design and a range of advanced features. It comes with a high-resolution Retina display, delivering vibrant and sharp visuals for album art, photos, and videos. The device is powered by a fast A12 Bionic chip, ensuring smooth performance for apps, games, and multitasking.

With storage options of 64GB, 128GB, and 256GB, the iPod Pro provides ample space for your music library, apps, and media. The device supports high-quality audio playback with support for various audio formats, making it a perfect companion for audiophiles. The iPod Pro also features a rear 8MP camera for capturing photos and 1080p HD videos, along with a front-facing 1.2MP FaceTime HD camera for video calls and selfies.

Connectivity options include Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 5.0, allowing for seamless streaming and pairing with wireless headphones and other devices. The iPod Pro is compatible with Apple Music and the App Store, giving you access to millions of songs, playlists, and a wide range of apps and games. Additionally, the device runs on iOS, providing a user-friendly interface and access to features like Siri, iMessage, and more.

The iPod Pro's battery life supports hours of music playback and video streaming, making it an ideal choice for entertainment on the go. Its lightweight and portable design ensure that you can take it anywhere, whether you're heading to the gym, traveling, or just relaxing at home.
